How to make the Perfect Cookie

The secret is just not too much flour then it won't be fluffy and good

Ingredients List

  • 1 1/4 cups All-Purpose Flour
  • 1 tsp Baking Soda
  • 1 tsp Salt
  • 1 cup Butter (softened/browned)
  • 3/4 cup Granulated Sugar
  • 3/4 cup Brown Sugar
  • 1 tsp Vanilla Extract
